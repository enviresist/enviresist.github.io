About

The contents provided on https://enviresist.github.io and the corresponding sub-pages is meant to support environmental research with a focus on antimicrobial resistance. The contents reflects work in progress and the information provided may not be sufficiently validated, misleading, or even wrong. Consequently, the provided information, is not intended to be used for any serious purpose or application without thorough validation by the user. In that regard, the liability disclaimer of TU Dresden, Germany, applies.

Projects

  • TEXAS: A JPI-AMR project on novel technologies for AMR surveillance
  • PNECR: An approach to the derivation of no-effect concentrations regarding selection for antibiotic resistance
